How to Play SF Spelling Bee

Overview

The SF Spelling Bee is a word puzzle game where players try to form as many words as possible in 45 seconds using a set of given letters. Here's how to play:

Rules

  1. Find words using the letters provided. You can not use the same circle twice in one word.
  2. Words must be at least three letters long.
  3. Insurance/State Farm terms are worth double points.
  4. There will be at least three insurance/State Farm terms in each attempt.
  5. Words score points based on length, one point per letter and two per letter for SF terms.
  6. Try to find as many words as possible and aim for the highest score!
  7. If you are in the top 10 you can record your name in the leaderboard!
