How to Play SF Wordle

Overview

SF Wordle is a word guessing game where you get 6 chances to guess a 4-6 letter word. Here's how to play:

Rules

  1. After making a guess the letter will be one of three colors.
  2. Green means that the letter is in the correct spot.
  3. Yellow means that letter is in the word but not that specific location.
  4. Gray means that letter isn't in the word at all.
  5. The words are all Insurance/State Farm related.
  6. If you guess the word before running out of tries you win!
