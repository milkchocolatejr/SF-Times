How to Play Connections

Overview

Connections is a word association game where players have to guess the connection between a set of words or phrases. Here's how to play:

Rules

  1. A series of words or phrases are presented to the player.
  2. You must select words in the same category.
  3. The connection could be a common theme, category, or concept that links the words together.
  4. You have limited lives so try not to guess randomly.
  5. If you get all four connections you win.

Example

For example, if the words are "Apple," "Orange," and "Banana," the connection could be "Fruits."
